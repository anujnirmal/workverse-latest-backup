You may have a quick question - How is this
different from a real internship?

Usually interns get menial tasks. And sometimes
the company you join may not really give you
the exposure or hands on training you need.
In the workverse, you’re joining as an employee
whose decisions matter and voice is heard.
“The boss” will also personally mentor you.
Therefore this experience gives a much
stronger head start!

In the simulation, you’ll play the role of an
entry-level Finance Professional at a
fictitious company- 'GS Associates’

Your goal at the end of this
30-day journey is to get promoted
and not get fired!

Like in the real world, the L&D department
of GS Associates will train you.

With training delivered via live expert
sessions & animated videos, you'll
quickly build the foundation
necessary to perform in
upcoming tasks &
challenges .

Test your training with exciting business tasks and challenges.
Work individually and in groups to achieve the
goals set out for you, your team,
and your employer.

Everything you do, say or don’t in this simulation
leads to a loss or gain in points.

These points add up to a final score that decides
whether you've done well enough to get promoted
or if your poor performance will get you fired
just like in real life!

Our algorithm also produces your unique Workplace
Intelligence Profile that ranks you on the ten
21st-century workplace skills employers seek.
Perform well here to gain an edge in
placement opportunities that we
will create for you!
