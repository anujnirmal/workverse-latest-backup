CURRICULUM ARCHITECT

APPLY

X Billion Skills Lab is an enterprise with a purpose! Unemployability is a looming socio-economic and humanitarian crisis for India and we're building a crack team to solve it.

The world of work is changing and millions are getting left behind Employers now demand "smart generalists" who are trained to apply skills like emotional intelligence, critical thinking, problem solving and storytelling to deliver outcomes at work beyond just their technical competence. In-fact the World economic forum lists these skills amongst the top 10 must haves for jobs of the future.

While at a high level everyone agrees that these generalist skills are essential - a hard truth is majority of India's 3.8 crore college students are enrolled in Category B & C institutions. Unfortunately, they lack the resources to provide high quality exposure to their students. As a result, such youth often get left behind in accessing aspirational career opportunities as compared to their counterparts from elite tier institutions.

What we've done: built a 21st century Softskills accelerator for Bharat

We distilled wisdom and experiences from 1000+ successful professionals to build a structured 50 hour e-learning journey that demonstrates college students how to apply their creative, social and emotional intelligence to deliver outcomes and tackle challenges at work. All content heavily leverages local contexts like bollywood, sport, current affairs and familiar role models. In summary, we're democratizing access to higher order skills.

After experiencing success and validation from 40+ colleges, 50,000+ learners and 50 public/private sector organizations - it's time to scale our impact.

We're expanding our core team to transform 1 million graduates into future ready smart generalists over the next 3 years.

  • Write for content formats such as Workplace hacks
  • Interview CEOs and experts for masterclasses on the platform
  • Collaborate with learning experience team to action learner feedback

Preferred prior experience: Business journalism, research or curriculum writing

TECHNICAL ARCHITECT

APPLY

X Billion Skills Lab enhances the employability of graduates in tier 2 & 3 cities for the new world of work. Our e-learning experience demonstrates workplace applications of 21st century skills such as emotional intelligence, creative problem solving, entrepreneurial mindset, remote collaboration and storytelling. Each learner receives access to animated modules, a personal mentor, masterclasses with CEOs and a remote experienceship. By the end each candidate is trained to apply their innate intelligence to successfully tackle 1000+ workplace scenarios and build a sustained growth trajectory.

Technical Skills
ReactJS, NodeJS, Kafka, Elasticsearch, GraphQL, PostgreSQL - Cloud: AWS, Digital Ocean

DevOps
Docker, PM2 and similar services. CI/CD deployments.

Experience
2years + and should have worked on scaling systems from 0 to 10k+ users

Attitude
- Willing to take ownership of the product, mission and vision
- Is keen to work at the intersection of marketing, design, customer experience and technology
- Can operate in environment with minimal resources

Every year millions graduate out of an outdated education system which fails to prepare them for the new world of work. As the nature of business evolves, companies seek talent who are able to apply their innate intelligence to deliver outcomes at work. They no longer care just about degrees. In-fact businesses are reducing their time sizes to only retain or hire those who can make measurable and sustained contributions to their business goals across all roles and levels. The mismatch in the expectations and volumes between demand & supply of jobs puts both sides at risk - laks of deserving youth not getting access to meaningful career opportunities and companies not being able to recruit talent required to achieve the set business goals and to deliver on debt or investment returns.

Want to be a part of an organization that transforms India's Youth?

About X Billion Skills Lab (XBSL)

At XBSL, we are on a mission to enhance the employability of graduates in Tier 2 & 3 colleges. Why should only candidates from elite colleges get access to aspirational career opportunities? Companies today seek talent proficient in higher order skills like communication, emotional intelligence, and problem-solving. In-fact the World Economic Forum ranks them as “must haves” for jobs of the future that survive automation. The problem is these skills cannot be taught through conventional lectures and most colleges largely still focus on just imparting academic / technical skills. So we’ve built an immersive and gamified workplace training simulator that puts learners in the middle of scenarios & challenges that they would have to tackle in real life! This gives our learners a unique edge in hiring and a strong foundation for rapid growth.


Your mission as our Corporate partnerships manager

At XBSL we don't just train students for jobs of the future, we also enable them to get aspirational opportunities. Imagine the impact we could create by placing ambitious youth from tier 2 & 3 colleges in emerging fin tech, edu tech and health tech startups. On the other hand, such companies are also looking for smart talent trained in applying higher order soft skills. Your mission as our corporate partnerships manager is to partner with the founders to engage new age start ups to hire learners from our pool.


Your key responsibilities are to:

1. Identify and build a rapport with startups who could be potential employers for our learners
2. Convince HR heads on how our assessment data on each candidate’s soft skills proficiency can enable them to make smart hiring decisions
3. Track performance and feedback of candidates trained & placed by us
4. Partner with the curriculum team to enhance learning experience based on feedback by employers


Prior Experience
  • No of Years:

    1-2 years

  • Experience:

    1 year (min)

  • Domain:

    Management consulting, Strategy, Business Development or Sales

  • Place of Residence:

    Mumbai (no remote work policy)

  • Job Type:

    Full Time

Write to us on hr@xbillionskillslab.com for more information.
